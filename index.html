T Drum Music

What We Do

T Drum Music is an independent, full service music consultancy run by three passionate music supervisors. Our specialities include:

Search

We call it ‘searches with personality’, combining our passions to bring a unique perspective to each job. Original, creative, imaginative, innovative, resourceful, we pride ourselves on going to great lengths to find the right fit for your film.

Composition

We take a non-exclusive approach, giving us the freedom to work with a broad range of professional composers, artists, choirs, orchestras, poets and everything in between to accommodate any bespoke brief.

Licensing

Coming from an in-house agency background we combine a breadth of licensing experience, including (but not limited to), advertising, film, television and videogames.

Who We Are

Person 1

Person 1 set up T Drum Music in October 2011 following 8 years experience in music supervision. Starting out as Creative Director of Huge Music, WCRS advertising agencies's in house music consultancy, Person 1 supervised a number of award winning advertising campaigns, feature films and television series.

Person 1 then joined Chrysalis Music as Creative Director of synchronisation and subsequently joined Spirit Music Group as Senior Director of Creative Services. These roles included creative searches, fee and deal term negotiation, issuing contracts and A&R scouting for the UK market.

xx@tmmusic.co.uk

Person 2

Working the last 10 years in music supervision, including six years at Soundtree Music, Adam has worked on some of the biggest campaigns of the last decade including; John Lewis ‘Clues’, Guinness ’Noitulove’ (Creative Circle Awards 2006, Best Use Of Music (Silver)) and 3 Mobile ’Tuperzik’ (D&AD Awards 2006, Best Use Of Music). He set up T Drum Music in 2011 with Person 1.

xx@tmmusic.co.uk

Person 3

Person 3 has worked in music synchronisation since 2004. He began his career with Soundtree Music where he spent 5 years and in that time oversaw numerous high profile projects for clients including Sony Walkman, Smirnoff and Person 3 amongst others. In 2009 he joined A&GSync as their UK sync manager and was responsible for the award winning Ikea 'Playing With My Friends' commercial, Samsung 'The Chase' and Axe 'The Chain'. Person 2 joined T Drum in July 2013.

xx@tmmusic.co.uk
